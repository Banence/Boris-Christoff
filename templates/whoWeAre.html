Our Story

Boris Hristov Foundation is registered as a public benefit foundation with the mission to preserve the creative heritage of Boris Hristov, to help talented artists, to organize and realize cultural events of high artistic value, to fight for the preservation of the fundamental and timeless achievements of Bulgarian culture and art.

The Foundation organizes and conducts concerts, master classes, donation campaigns and seminars, as well as contributes to the presentation and realization of Bulgarian artists in the country and abroad.

In the leadership of the Foundation are Stoyan Bonchev, Nikolay Angelov and the world-famous tenor Maestro Boyko Tsvetanov - the first and most successful disciple of the great Boris Hristov.

In recent years, the Boris Hristov Foundation has held a series of celebrations, donation campaigns, events and concerts related to the 100th anniversary of Maestro Hristov.
